GrelinTB

I coded the first version when I had time during a 16-day vacation. Because default Tkinter was looking really bad also I was bored and wanted to learn something new.
So GrelinTB was born, I really like CustomTkinter and I also realized the power of object oriented programming.
Since I code object-oriented, everything except the changelogs and the license is in the whole window. It looks much more beautiful.
Until v1.2.0.0 there were 2 disadvantages compared to MetterXP, one was fixed with v1.2.0.0. The other one was fixed with v1.3.0.0. Finally there are no disadvantages compared to MetterXP.
