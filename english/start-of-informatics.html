Start Of Informatics

In late 2021, I started looking for other operating systems due to the problems my family was having with our then 10-year-old laptop. And that's when I discovered GNU/Linux distributions.

First I used Linux Mint Xfce, which was actually very good, but it still had one problem, the WiFi. After that I became a distrohopper, sometimes I would switch to a new distribution every 5 minutes. By the way thanks Samsung to nice SSD. :D

Then I was searching for something and I saw Ramkafasi's Terminalden kurtulun project. The project obviously got me interested in coding. One of the reasons for this was both the curiosity of how it works and the huge logo on the interface.

I started to build BetterXP based on Terminalden kurtulun. This was my first project. But I remember I was writing the code with a text editor because of my ignorance at the time. You can click here for later.
