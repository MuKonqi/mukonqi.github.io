I spent most of my childhood interested in cars, I love cars, but I'm not interested in them as much as I used to be.

In early 2021, I signed up for the "technology" forum, which I will not name now and which I no longer use. I was already there for 3 months. In fact, at first I was more interested in phones than computers.
When my 10-year-old laptop started having problems, I started looking for other operating systems. That's when I discovered GNU/Linux distributions.
I installed Linux Mint Xfce, the first distro I used, on February 24, 2021. But I wanted to sail in this sea, so I became a distrohopper.

On March 6, 2021, I tried Terminalden kurtulun by Ramkafasi. I felt like doing something similar, so I developed BetterXP based on it.
In 2022 I played more games and increased the number of my projects.
But the bad thing about 2021-2022 was that I was a GNU fanboy... I had read on the GNU page that it was wrong to call Linux GNU/Linux, and it affected me a lot. This fanboyism lasted for a long time.
I also opened my Steam account, first bought ETS2, then my library grew and grew, and after switching to Steam dollars I bought generic games from Epic Games (which has the worst launcher I've ever seen).

The year 2023 was a bit too empty in terms of projects due to the LGS exam, the biggest advances were MetterXP v3.0 version and this site. I was playing games when I had time from classes.

In 2024, my main project will be GrelinTB using the CustomTkinter library, I plan to release it soon. I also plan to learn languages like C++ and C#.
