LGS Process

I enrolled in a course for LGS in the 2rd quarter of 2022 and I didn't study until September 2022 when I don't study in full LGS format because I was only in 7th grade then.

When we came to September 2022, that was the time for serious study. At first, I was solving 1200-1400 questions with great enthusiasm, but the books I solved were not very new generation. After that, my question count started to drop as time went on, but I never stopped solving questions except for important exceptions. After the first times, my question count was between 700 and 900 (usually it was closer to 700 :D) but I was solving more new generation style books. In the last weeks, I started solving essays and that's when the real boredom started. I was really fed up then.

As for the result, it was a very good result. At first, of course thanks to my family. Thanks to all my teachers in this process. Thanks to my 2 friends at school and my group of friends in the course.
