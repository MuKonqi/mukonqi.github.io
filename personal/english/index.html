I love developing simple projects (like this website, Nottodbox and GrelinTB), playing computer video games.

As for things which my favorites, my favorite color is #376296, my favorite distribution is opensuse Tumbleweed and my favorite DE is KDE Plasma 6.

I'm interested in vehicles and technology.

Childhood

When I was a kid, my main hobby was cars, but now I'm not as interested as I used to be.
